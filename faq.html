Magazines – How do I know which ones I’ll proceed?

Fill this form here and we wille contact you asap.

Confirmation – How do I know my subscription is live?

You will receive a confirmation email from us within 24 hours of your subscription, with all the details of your subscription and a link to your portal, allowing you to check and update your information.

Overseas – Do you offer international work?

We have a different headquarters anywhere in the world. Just select your country from the drop down menu when you subscribe and we’ll update the your position according to your needs.

I'm already a subscriber

Project - How do I team up with another developer?

You can get in touch online at any time. Simply sign in using your email address and password, or if you don’t have an account set up, you can register using the email address associated with your subscription. Alternatively, just email us and we’ll be happy to help.

Changing address – How do I update my address details?

You can amend your details online. Simply sign in using your email address and password, or if you don’t have an account set up, you can register using the email address associated with your subscription. Alternatively, just email us and we’ll be happy to help.

Opting out – What if I don’t want to receive the newsletter?

We know that some people might not want to receive all the newsletter, so it’s easy to opt out. For example, if you already subscribe to the newsletter, just email us to let us know and we’ll make sure we unsubscribe you. We recommend to subscribe tho.

I’m just curious

Offers – How can I find out about new portfolio and project?

You can subscribe to the shop mailing list here to be the first to know about new project or developers you listed as interesting. You can also follow us on Instagram to get selected updates on our community.

Subscriptions – Can I subscribe without login in?

No — the shop is intended as a quick and easy way to connect the world’s best independent developer with no commitments. If you’d like to get to know more by subscription, take a look at our monthly subscription service.

Gifts – Can I subscribe for somebody else?

You certainly can. If you’d like to send us his/her portfolio before, simply enter your login’s name and shipping address at checkout and we’ll send the link directly to them.

I have a different question

Distribution – How can I have my magazine considered for Stack?

We’re always looking for new magazines to add to the Stack selection. If you’d like to have your magazine considered for Stack, please send a copy to us at Stack, Makerversity, Somerset House, London WC2R 1LA.

Internships – Do you take work experience placements?

Not currently – if that changes at any point in the future we’ll announce it on the Stack blog.

Events – Where can I find information about upcoming events?

You can find details of upcoming Stack Live events at our Eventbrite page. For all other Stack-related events keep an eye on the blog.
